Site description

This site is intended to help Fathers get involved with the day-to-day activities and lifestyle maintenance of their households. With Moms working more and focusing on careers, managing the household is a multi-parent task and Dads have to step-up to the plate, literally! Preparing meals and washing dishes! It's time for Dads to roll-up their sleeves and take on the tasks historically and culturally designed for women. With the times changing men are enlisted to pick up the slack in the household and this website will assist men on that journey.
